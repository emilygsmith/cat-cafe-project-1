Welcome to Cardiff's home of cats, cake and coffee!

About Us

We are an independent cat cafe based in Cardiff, Wales. Join us for a delicious homemade cake and a coffee, choosing from our large range of hot and cold drinks. We have a number of cute kitties hanging around to join you for your morning coffee, late lunch and everything inbetween, with some even looking for their forever homes!

Our Mission

We work in partnership with Cardiff's largest cat rescue, Cat Friends, with the cafe providing an environment for our lovely rescue cats to adjust to the company of humans once more - and maybe even find their forever homes. There is a small menu available to purchase some additional treats for our rescue kitties, and is a great way to make them comfortable with you and maybe even allow a pet or two!
